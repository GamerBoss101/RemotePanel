Here are some reasons why you might not be able connect.

  • MCSS is not running (locally on port 25560)
  • The API url you entered is incorrect
  • Your firewall is blocking the connection
  • Your browser extentions are blocking the connection
  • Misconfigured portforwarding / ngrok / remote.it
